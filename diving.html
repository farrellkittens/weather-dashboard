Useful Additions

Worth adding once the core page is settled
Dive-window score: combine tide stage, swell, wind, daylight, and lightning into a quick go/no-go trend.
Slack-water timing: especially useful for channels, bridges, jetties, and high-current shore dives.
Surf zone note: wave height plus wind-wave chop can matter more than offshore swell at entries and exits.
Recent buoy trend: actual buoy readings can catch bad viz or wind faster than a model forecast.
Sunrise, sunset, and moon: helpful for night dives and early-morning entries.
Manual local report: a small editable note field for shop/club reports when no public feed exists.
Data: Open-Meteo marine, weather, and air quality APIs; NOAA CO-OPS tide predictions where a station is set.
